About

YAKAMOZ is a creative technology studio exploring the boundary between design, data, and digital art. Our name, YAKAMOZ, can most closely be translated as the moonlight’s reflection on water, and we hope our work brings that same reflection of beauty of your vision to the digital world.

We create interactive visuals, generative systems, and custom tools that combine aesthetics with purpose. Our goal is to build experiences that are as thoughtful as they are innovative.

YAKAMOZ was founded as a space for experimentation, collaboration, and exploring how design and code can reflect the complexity of the world around us.
